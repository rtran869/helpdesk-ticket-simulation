Help Desk Ticket Simulation & Support Lab

Simulated Tier 1 IT Service Desk environment designed to replicate real-world troubleshooting, ticket management, escalation workflows, and support documentation.

📊 Project Overview

  • 25 simulated IT support tickets
  • Tier 1 troubleshooting workflows
  • Escalation logic and resolution tracking
  • Excel-based metrics dashboard

🛠 Skills Demonstrated

  • IT Service Desk Ticket Management
  • Hardware & Software Troubleshooting
  • User Support & Issue Resolution
  • Incident Documentation
  • Escalation Procedures
  • Excel Reporting & Metrics Tracking

📁 Project Structure

  • data/ – 25-ticket dataset (Excel)
  • dashboard/ – resolution metrics dashboard
  • docs/ – troubleshooting workflow & sample tickets

📌 Example Ticket Types

  • Password resets & account lockouts
  • Printer connectivity issues
  • Outlook/email access problems
  • VPN and network troubleshooting

📈 Key Metrics Tracked

  • Average resolution time
  • Ticket volume by issue type
  • Escalation rate
  • Closed vs open tickets
